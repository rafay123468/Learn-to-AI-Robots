Summary on AI Robots

AI Robots are machines that combine Artificial Intelligence (AI) with robotics. Unlike traditional robots, AI robots can think, learn, and make decisions instead of just following pre-programmed command.

Key Features of AI Robots

Perception → They use sensors, cameras, and microphones to understand their surroundings.
Decision-Making → AI algorithms help them analyze data and choose the best action.
Learning Ability → With Machine Learning, they improve performance over time.
Autonomy → They can perform tasks with little or no human intervention.

Types of AI Robots

1.Industrial Robots Used in factories for assembling, packaging, welding, etc.
2. Service Robots Help in hospitals, hotels, and customer service.
3. Humanoid Robots Designed to look and act like humans (e.g., Sophia).
4. Autonomous Robots Self-driving cars, delivery drones.
5. Military & Space Robots Used for defense, exploration, and risky missions.
